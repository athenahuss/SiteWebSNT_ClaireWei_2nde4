Mon Parcours

Voici mon parcours artistique et personnel.

Mes intérêts et expériences sont variés et enrichissants. Je m'implique au niveau scolaire en tant que déléguée de classe, représentante au CVL au Collège.

En dehors de l'école, je m'implique dans mes oeuvres artistiques dont j'en suis très fière.

Voici une exhibition des plus récentes.

2025

Metamorphosis

"Metamorphosis" is an artistic project in which I created a self-portrait that blends human and animal features, using pencil as the primary medium. Throughout this project, I explored different drawing techniques, including shading, proportions, and texture rendering. This project allowed me to deepen my understanding of pencil drawing while developing a more expressive and imaginative approach to a "regular" auto portrait.

2023

REM

With the arrival of the new train (REM) and the yearbook theme centered around trains, I chose to create a watercolor and pen illustration with a collage-style visual, incorporating calligraphy elements. Through this project, I explored how to capture machinery from different perspectives and gained a deeper understanding of the train's structure and components. This experience helped me develop my skills in technical drawing, composition, and mixed-media artwork.

2022

Watercolor Bear

Watercolor studies with real life pictures, I explored different watercolor techniques such as diluating the colors and especially student contrasting and even recreating the blurry effects.
